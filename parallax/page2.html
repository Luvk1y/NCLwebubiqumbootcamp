General Information

The NorCal Time Trial Series will hold at least one event per month on a
track such as Sonoma Raceway, Thunderhill Raceway, or WeatherTech
Raceway at Laguna Seca. Full safety equipment is required,
and your car must pass technical inspection before you can compete.
